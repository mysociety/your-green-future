Enfield’s carbon challenges

These are the top two contributors to emissions in the Enfield area, based on the latest government data.

Housing

Housing is responsible for 42% of Enfield’s emissions – higher than average in the UK.

What needs to change

2,592 heat pumps will need to be installed each year to meet 2030 targets.

5,848 homes a year will need insulation upgrades to ensure all homes are properly insulated by 2035.

Transport

Transport is responsible for 34% of Enfield’s emissions.

What needs to change

Enfield needs to install at least 1,096 electric vehicle charging points by 2030.

The proportion of people commuting by low-carbon options needs to rise from 51% to 80%, meaning massive investment in transport infrastructure.
